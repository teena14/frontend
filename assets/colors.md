# Visual Rules

The website should feel calm and premium.

Use generous whitespace.

Avoid strong gradients.

Avoid heavy shadows.

Avoid colorful backgrounds.

Yellow should attract attention only where necessary.

Dark colors should be used to create contrast rather than dominate the page.

The overall appearance should feel elegant, minimal, technical, and timeless.

Think Linear, Vercel, Apple and Stripe rather than typical AI startup websites.
